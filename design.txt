Main elements:
    - navbar: logo, info and buttons to select between 'market' and 'players' mode.
    - core:
        - a sidebar to choose from the available analysis.
        - a panel where the selected analysis is presented.
        - another sidebar with a Twitter football feed.
        - a blocking modal for login.

Session variables:
    - market_data: the history of transactions obtained after login.
    - players_data: the player list obtained after login.
    - credentials_data: the user Biwenger credentials.
